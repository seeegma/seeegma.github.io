1854 George Haskell Train from Utah to San Bernardino

The George Haskell Train from Utah to San Bernardino was “one of the largest covered wagon trains ever to make the trip.”1 It departed from Utah County, Utah in March 1854, and arrived in San Bernardino on June 8th.2
