FAN List

This is a list of 65 of some of Robert A. Poppett’s family, friends, associates, and neighbors. With them, he crossed the Atlantic and the Great Plains - did freighting, mining, ranching, and real estate – celebrated holidays and social occasions – joined fraternal organizations, and became family as their children and grandchildren intermarried.
